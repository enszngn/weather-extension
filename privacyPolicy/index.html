Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

We believe that your personal information belongs to you. This extension is designed to be simple, useful, and fully respectful of your privacy.

Location Information

To show you the correct weather information, the extension uses your device's location. This location is sent to the weather website to fetch the forecast for your area.

No Data Tracking

We do not track your browsing history, we do not show advertisements, and we do not collect, store, or sell any of your personal details.

Settings

Any configuration choices or settings you select are saved locally on your own computer and are never shared with anyone.
